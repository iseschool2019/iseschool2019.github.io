Welcome

The Intelligent Software Engineering Winter School brings together academics, researchers, practitioners, and graduate students to acquire essential knowledge and valuable skills for applying machine learning techniques to tackle software engineering challenges. The School is hosted by the Software Engineering Research Lab at Zhejiang University, which is one of the leading universities in China. Zhejiang University is actively involved in software engineering academic community and made significant progress in the research field. The school runs from Nov 25 to 27, 2019 in Hangzhou, China.

Lectures are given by world-leading experts in the areas of Data Science, Software Analytics and Machine Learning. The lectures are combined with hands-on experience on various practical topics including Mining Software Repositories, Qualitative Data Analysis, and Automated Software Engineering. The objective of this school is to give young researchers in China as well as from globe opportunities to interact closely and intensively with the world-leading software engineering experts. We expect this school will benefit the attendees, the community, and eventually promote the advance of intelligent software engineering in the world.

About ISESchool 2019
